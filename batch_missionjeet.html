DRONA NEET Class 11th Batch Details

1.⁠ ⁠We'll cover class 11th  Physics, Chemistry, and Biology for students preparing for the NEET 2028 examination.

2.⁠ ⁠All lectures will be conducted Live Online, and in case you miss a session, recordings will be available to students on the Mission JEET App/Website.

3.⁠ ⁠Classes will be scheduled in a structured timetable to ensure systematic NEET preparation.

4.⁠ ⁠There will be two classes per day, and classes will be conducted 6 days a week.

5.⁠ ⁠PDF Notes of each class will be uploaded after the lecture on the Mission JEET Application.

6.⁠ ⁠Daily Practice Problems (DPPs) with Video Solutions by the Faculty will be provided to improve problem-solving ability and conceptual understanding.

7.⁠ ⁠Chapterwise Practice Sheets / Modules (NEET Level) pdfs will be provided for comprehensive practice.

8.⁠ ⁠A summary lecture of each chapter will be provided after chapter completion for quick revision and concept clarity.

9.⁠ ⁠Biweekly scheduled tests will be conducted according to the test planner to monitor your preparation progress.

10.⁠ ⁠Specialized NEET Pattern Tests will also be conducted to evaluate your preparation level.

11.⁠ ⁠To support your Board preparation along with NEET, free access to the Prarambh Science Batch will be provided.

12.⁠ ⁠After completion of the syllabus, dedicated revision classes will be conducted for strong exam preparation.

13.⁠ ⁠Classes will commence on 6th April 2026, and the complete syllabus will be covered by 31st January 2027.

14.⁠ ⁠The revision phase will continue until 28th February 2027, followed by tests until 15th March 2027.

15.⁠ ⁠The entire course will remain accessible to you until the NEET 2028 examination.

16.⁠ ⁠This batch is non-refundable and non-transferable
